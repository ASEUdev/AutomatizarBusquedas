A note about Pc searches


Delay (in seconds)

Set the minimum and maximum time between each search. A random number will be selected within that range.

The randomness will make your searches appear more organic.


Maximum delay cannot be below your minimum delay

Minimum delay cannot be below 0

Delay cannot exceed 290 seconds

Bing may not properly track searches made less than 1 second apart
